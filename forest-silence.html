Forest's Silence

Deep within the woods, silence reigns. Not the absence of sound, but a presence — alive and ancient. Trees whisper secrets in the breeze, and the sun filters through leaves like golden lace. It’s in this quiet we find ourselves again.

The scent of moss, the crunch of leaves underfoot, the occasional rustle — all speak a language older than words. A walk in the forest is not an escape but a return, a reconnection with the rhythm of nature.
