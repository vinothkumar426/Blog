Ocean Echo

The ocean speaks in echoes, whispering stories of ancient tides and moonlit dreams. Its voice crashes and retreats, never the same, always familiar.

Each wave is a breath, drawn deep from the earth’s soul. In its rhythm, we hear peace — the silence between roars, the stillness after storms.

To stand by the shore is to hear the universe hum. The ocean doesn’t just exist — it listens, it calls, it heals.
