Rainy Harmony

Rain brings a rhythm that quiets the chaos. It falls like a lullaby, soft and slow, washing over everything with a sense of calm and clarity.

Walking in the rain, the world feels hushed. Every droplet is a note in nature’s song—a symphony of stillness and reflection.

In the harmony of rain, we are reminded that growth often comes in gentle waves and peace in silent showers.
