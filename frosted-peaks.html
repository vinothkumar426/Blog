Frosted Peaks

Towering into the sky, the frosted peaks wear a crown of snow that sparkles like crushed diamonds in the morning sun. The silence of the heights is a song in itself—unspoken, but deeply felt.

These mountains, weathered yet proud, remind us of resilience. Their snowy slopes echo strength, grace, and a timeless endurance that humbles all who wander near.

Standing before the frosted peaks, we are reminded: some beauty comes only with cold, only with stillness, only with time.
