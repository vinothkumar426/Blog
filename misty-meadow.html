Misty Meadow

The morning dew clings to blades of grass, painting the meadow in a soft, silvery glow. Fog dances gently through the field, wrapping nature in a delicate veil.

The world hushes in the presence of mist. Birds sing softer. Wind moves slower. And in the heart of the meadow, time pauses—offering a moment of pure serenity.

Misty mornings are nature’s poetry—written not in ink, but in silence and breath.
